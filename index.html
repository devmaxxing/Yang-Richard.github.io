Welcome to Live 613.

Live 613 is an android app that connects you to the most interesting events that Ottawa has to offer. All the information you will need to get to any event around the city will be right at your fingertips.

Get the App on Android

Explore the nations capital to the fullest.

Simple, convenient, and a one-stop shop for all the information you need about the events. The modern day equivalent of Varys, the spider, from Game of Thrones.

  • See for yourself what your peers are saying about the events through pictures and tweets.
  • Easily find all the information that you'll need to know to get to the event and have a great time.
  • View the event on a map of Ottawa and instantly get directions from where you are.
  • Save the event to your phone and get calendar alerts as the date gets closer.
